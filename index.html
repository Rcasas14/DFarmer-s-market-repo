Company Profile

D’Farmers Market Fruits and Pastries Center is a family owned agricultural business located at Purok 8 Cawayan, Calinan District, Davao City. Its agricultural farm measures around 14 hectares with durian fruit trees as the main crop with mangosteen, lanzones, jackfruit, marang, guyabano, rambutan, avio, papaya, and coconut trees as intercrops.

Our Vision

We, the D’Farmers Market Fruits and Pastries Center envisions to be the leading processor of frozen fruits, produced and grown by farmers thereby increasing healthy living and employment.

Processing Procedure

1. Receiving

RECEIVING

Fruits are received at the receiving area. Personnel to receive the durian should always be accompanied by a Quality Inspector to check the quality of the raw materials in accordance to the Raw Material Specification.

Dfarmers Market

2. Sorting

SORTING

Ripe fruits are sorted out from unripe.

Dfarmers Market

3. Spraying

SPRAYING

Fruits are washed with chlorinated water using a sprayer to clean, remove dirt and avoid transport of foreign matter inside the processing area.

Dfarmers Market

4. Soaking

SOAKING

Previously cleaned fruits are then soaked in chlorinated water with specific time and concentration.

Dfarmers Market

5. Breaking

BREAKING

Production personnel with good personal hygiene, wearing proper protective equipment breaks the fruits using clean and sanitized stainless steel breaking equipment.

Dfarmers Market

6.Processing

PROCESSING

Fruits are sorted accordingly for export and local quality.

Dfarmers Market

7. FILING INTO FOOD TRAYS

FILING INTO FOOD TRAYS

Sorted fruits are arranged into clean and sanitized fruit trays.

Dfarmers Market

8. WEIGHING

WEIGHING

Fruits in fruit trays are weighed in a calibrated weighing scale where each tray should weigh as per costumer specification.

Dfarmers Market

9. TRAYING

TRAYING

Weighed fruits in fruit trays are arranged into clean and sanitized trolley.

Dfarmers Market

10. Pre-Cooling

PRE-COOLING

Fruits are sorted accordingly for export and local quality.

Dfarmers Market

11. BLAST FREEZING

BLAST FREEZING

Products are blast freeze for certain time at -40°C depending on the kind of fruit being process.

Dfarmers Market

12. VACUUM PACKING

VACUUM PACKING

Blast frozen products are forwarded to packing area for vacuum packing, sealing and coding.

Dfarmers Market

13. FILING INTO CRATES

FILING INTO CRATES

Vacuum packed products are arranged into clean, sanitized and dried crates.

Dfarmers Market

14. COLD STORING

COLD STORING

Finished products are then forwarded to cold storage with temperature of at least -18oC. Products are off of the floor using pallets.

Dfarmers Market
